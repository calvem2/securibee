our mission

With big tech companies hiding behind complicated privacy agreements and explanations on computer security topics being overly technical, navigating the internet can be intimidating and scary.

Our passions lie at the intersection of technology, education, and social justice. We wanted to pursue a capstone project that aims to educate folx on how to go about navigating the internet safely. The securi-bee website and extension is built for the everyday user, presenting the buzz on computer security in a way that everyone can understand -- no hyper-technical language or jargon.
