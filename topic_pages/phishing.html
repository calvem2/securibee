How to recognize email phishing attempts.

About

Email phishing is a type of scam where attackers pose as official organizations or someone that you know whether that be from work or one of your relatives to gain access to your personal information.

Email phishing can catch users off guard, so it is best to always practice caution when viewing emails from any source.

This type of attack is one of the most dangerous because users often fall for the social engineering the attacker uses in order for the user to take the bait.

Why you should care

Your information is at risk when becoming subject to a phishing attack.

Tools & Tips

Check out these tips to avoid falling for email phishing traps.

Check email address of the sender

account_box

Does the email have an official website signatures (i.e. @company.org) or is there something off about it?

Look out for the slightest of misspellings in the sender's address.

It is extremely easy for attackers to change the displayed name on a sent email so don't rely on just that fact when receiving emails.

Beware of imposters

face

An attacker could gain access to the email account of one of your friends, family members, or coworkers and pose as them to get you to click on a malicious link.

If your someone you know sends you a link to a "funny video" or something else that urgently needs to be clicked it is usually a good idea to check with them via phone call or text to make sure that they actually sent the link and it was not someone who gained access to their account.

Misspellings and bad grammar

assignment

If you receive an email from a seemingly official source then make sure that the email doesn't have any misspellings or bad grammar.

An easy way to check for this is to simply copy and paste any of the text that is not hyperlinked into the document editor of your choice to easily see where the grammatical errors lie.

Create filter for phishing emails

Some but not all phishing emails will get sent to your spam or junk folder before they get sent to your inbox.
